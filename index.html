Books and Teaching

  • Cummings, et. al. Applied Computational Aerodynamics: A Modern Engineering Approach, Cambridge University Press, 2015.
    • This textbook provides clear and concise information about the state of computational aerodynamics (CA), its applications, and its importance.
      It's a very pleasant read for anyone aspiring to learn CA.
  • Davidson, P.A. Turbulence: An Introduction for Scientists and Engineers, Oxford Universoty Press, 2015.
    • Another rather enjoyable book that discusses all of the ins and outs of turbulence. Its not exactly for the faint of heart, but it's well worth
      a read.
  • Wilcox, D.C. Turbulence Modeling for CFD, 1993.
