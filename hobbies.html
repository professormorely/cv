My Hobbies


  1. Video Games

    I've played video games since I was a little kid. As an only child, they offered endless hours of fun.
    As I grew older, they offered a way for me to make friends outside of my hometown and across the world
    and introduced me to the idea of constant and consistent improvement.
    Since high school, I have almost exclusively played video games with high skill curves. In high school,
    it that came first in the form of GunZ the Duel, a glitchy and twitch third person shooter, and Starcraft,
    which was argueably the first real eSport. I still enjoy playing both games, though these days I mainly
    play the original Starcraft's successor, Starcraft 2, with friends back in the US.
